# Legal and distribution gate

**Status: not completed legal advice.** No public binary or format-support
claim may be made from this bootstrap.

Before a public release, record a dated review covering:

1. the selected Qt GPL/LGPL/commercial route and its distribution obligations;
2. H.264/AAC patent considerations in each intended jurisdiction;
3. reliance on Apple and Microsoft system frameworks;
4. all redistributed dependencies, notices, source hashes, and SBOM;
5. the explicit absence of FFmpeg, NodeAV, libav components, and bundled codec
   binaries; and
6. the published supported-format/legal matrix for the exact release.

Until that dated review explicitly approves fixture redistribution, generated
H.264, AAC, HEVC, AV1, MP4, MOV, M4A, and WebM artifacts remain local or in
controlled evidence storage. The public repository may contain generator
source, recipes, manifests, hashes, and small uncompressed oracle data.

This document is a release blocker and engineering checklist. It does not give
legal advice or certify any distribution as compliant.
